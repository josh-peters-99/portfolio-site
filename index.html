I'm a software engineer based in Utah. I write most of my code in Go and focus on building systems from scratch ― compilers, interpreters, text editors, databases, and developer tools.

I graduated with a degree in Computer Science. During my undergrad, I worked as a Computer Science Tutor and as a QA Software Engineer Intern. I currently work as a software engineer in the industrial automation space.

My goal is to master software engineering fundamentals by building foundational systems and core tools from the ground up.
